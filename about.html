About Us

ABA Biotech is an emerging ISO certified company and we are working as distributor of fine chemicals, MB grade chemicals, Culture Media, Nano product, Kit, Sequencing, Next Generation Sequencing(NGS), Plastic ware, Glassware, Lab consumables, Benchtop equipment etc.

We have been working in the market since the past nine years. We fill in collectively in understanding the need of the clients, keeping each other's business interest flawless so we could convey items at the correct time and at the most aggressive rates.
